About Me

My name is Aidan Sawyer. I'm a Software Engineering major and Philosophy Minor at the Rochester Institute of Technology.

My goal and purpose on GitHub and in my career is to contribute to and create projects that serve to increase the efficiency, speed, and capacity of the engine of scholarship and knowledge production that drives human achievement.

My current implementation of that goal has me working with various tools to assist with library automation tasks centered around digitization and description. These include: dublin-core-text-parser, organon, crosswalk, and lib-name-parser.
